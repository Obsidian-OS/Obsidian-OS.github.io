ObsidianOS

The Arch-based distro with A/B partitioning

A/B Partitioning

With A/B partitioning, updates are atomic. If an update fails, you can simply reboot into the previous partition, ensuring your system is always bootable.

Arch-Based

Enjoy the power and flexibility of Arch Linux, with access to the latest software and a massive repository of packages through the AUR.

Stable and Reliable

Built on the stability of ext4, ObsidianOS provides a reliable computing experience without the complexities of other filesystems.
